The Beginning

FAFCODE started as a personal challenge to learn modern web development. What began as simple HTML pages has evolved into a comprehensive learning project.

This project represents months of dedication, countless hours of coding, debugging, and learning from mistakes. It's not just a website - it's a testament to the learning journey.
